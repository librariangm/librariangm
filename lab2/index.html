How to Access an Ebook

By George Martinez


Step 1: Search the OneSearch box on the Martin Luther King, Jr. Library Homepage

You can access the library homepage here: Martin Luther King, Jr. Library

Type a few keywords related to your topic in the OneSearch box and click the icon of the magnifying glass to the right of the search box to initiate your search.

Step 2: Select Filters

Select the filters by clicking on the words “Online” and “Books.”

You can find the filters listed on the left of your search results. The filters help us to narrow our results to ebooks.

info In the future, as needed, you can also choose to add on more filters such as the ones listed below.

  • Publication Date
  • Subject
  • Library

Step 3: Browse Results

Review your results to see if there is an ebook you’re interested in learning more about.

Step 4: Click an Ebook Title to Learn More

Select a title and scroll to the “Summary” section of the page to determine if the book covers topics you would like to read about.

Step 5: View the Ebook Online

Once you have determined that you have found a title you would like to read, click on the link located in the “View Online/Full text availability” section of the page.
